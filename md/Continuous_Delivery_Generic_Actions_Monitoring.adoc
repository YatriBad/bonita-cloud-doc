= Monitoring a job

== Last job result information

The result of the last job is displayed next to the job:

* image:images/JobStatusInProgress.png[Inprogress]: Job is in progress
* image:images/JobStatusOK.png[JobOK]: Last job was successful
* image:images/JobStatusFailed.png[JobKO]: Last job was unsuccessful

=== Console Output

You can check the different actions and logs done in a job through the "console output". This output can be view during or after the job execution.

. Open the corresponding job by clicking on its title:

image::cloud/images/master/OpenAJob.gif[]

// [OpenAJob](images/OpenAJob.gif)

. Open the corresponding job instance by click on its ID in the main list or side list

image::cloud/images/master/OpenJobInstance.png[]

// [OpenJobInstance](images/OpenJobInstance.png)

. On the side panel, click on the "Console Output" entry:

image::cloud/images/master/ConsoleOutputAction.png[]

// [ConsoleOutput](images/ConsoleOutputAction.png)

. You will see all the different actions and logs done during the job:

image::cloud/images/master/ConsoleOutputEx.gif[,100%]

// [ConsoleOutputEx](images/ConsoleOutputEx.gif)
